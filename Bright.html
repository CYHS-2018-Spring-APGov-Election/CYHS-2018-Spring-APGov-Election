OUR MISSION

Our goal is to create unity, improve quality of life, and obtain equal opportunity for all Americans. We are committed to being transparent, inclusive, and informed in order to achieve a bright future for our country.




OUR VIEWS

Immigration

"Immigrants" should not be synonymous with "foreigners." To ensure the safety and security of our country, more extensive background checks should be implemented but the legal immigration process should not take nearly as long as it currently does.

Gun Control

Further gun control than what is currently implemented is strongly needed. More extensive background checks and proper licensing must be obtained, but increased regulations and enforcement will also need to be added (such as black market crackdowns).

Abortion

Abortion must remain legal and not be subject to removal from state governments. However, in order to prevent abortions, sex education classes should be adopted by all public schools.

Unemployment

Since 2009, the amount of high school graduates who attend college has considerably decreased due to the students staying home to work and support their families. However, jobs that do not require higher education often only pay minimum wage, which restricts many families from making ends meet. Citizens should not be bound to this lifestyle, so minimum wage should be slightly raised and the government should continue to provide jobs for the public.

Healthcare

The government having complete control of healthcare heightens concern that the government may monopolize the healthcare industry all together, and the government's current healthcare regulations are too extensive. The government should have less involvement in the healthcare field, allowing for individuals to make their own medical decisions, increase breakthroughs in the medical field, and will reduce the cost of healthcare for Americans.
